Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AU Journey. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you sign in using Microsoft Authentication.
  • Location Data: Your location is used only for navigation and routing features. It is not stored or shared.
  • Achievements: Achievement progress is stored locally on your device for your own tracking.

3. Web Content & Caching

AU Journey uses a WebView to display interactive campus maps and 3D content powered by three.js. We cache some content locally for offline use. This cached data does not include personal information and is stored only on your device.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to provide app features like navigation and badge collection. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or analytics.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • three.js: An open-source 3D rendering library for map visualization. It does not collect or process personal data.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal data on our servers.

Important: Your achievements are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all collected achievements will be permanently lost. Currently, AU Journey does not provide cloud sync or backup for achievements.

7. No Ads or Tracking

AU Journey does not include advertisements and does not use analytics or tracking tools.
